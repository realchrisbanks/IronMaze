Iron Maze — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Iron Maze (“the app”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use the Iron Maze iPhone and Apple Watch apps.

The short version

Iron Maze does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We have no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

Motion & sensor data

The game uses your Apple Watch’s motion sensors (accelerometer/gyroscope) to control the ball. This data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, transmitted, or stored.

Game recordings

In Compete mode, the app records the path of your ball during a run so you can replay and share it. These recordings are transferred only between your own paired Apple Watch and iPhone using Apple’s on-device connectivity, and are stored only on your devices. We never receive them. If you choose to share a recording, it is sent using the standard iOS share sheet to the destination you select; what happens after that is governed by that app or service.

Purchases

Iron Maze Pro is an optional one-time in-app purchase handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not see or store your payment information. Purchase verification is performed on-device using Apple’s StoreKit framework.

Children

Iron Maze does not collect any data and is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
